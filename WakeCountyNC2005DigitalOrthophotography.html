Description:
Abstract:
1"=100' true color digital Orthophotos with a 0.5' ground pixel resolution were produced for Wake County, NC
Purpose:
The dataset includes updated digital Orthophotography for the County's GIS Database. The primary tasks focused on new color aerial photography, scanning to digital imagery, primary and Q/C ground control, ortho only DEM update, the production of new color digital Orthophotos, and a conversion of all color Orthophotos to a black and white dataset. The project area covers all of Wake County and a 750' buffer.
Supplemental_Information:
The update portion of this project involved the production 1"=100' true color radiophotograph with a 0.5' ground pixel resolution. All photogrammetric mapping products meet the National Standards for Spatial Digital Accuracy (NSSDA) horizontal accuracy standards for 1"=100' mapping. The Limiting RMSE (Root-Mean-Square-Error) for X or Y is 1.0 foot.
